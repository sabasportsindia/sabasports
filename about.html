15+ Years of Excellence in Sports Manufacturing

SABA SPORTS is a proud Indian manufacturer and trader of premium quality sports equipment. Based in Meerut, U.P., we have been delivering trust and performance for over 15 years. Our journey started with a passion to create reliable carrom boards and cricket bats, and today we offer a full range of sports gear.

We blend traditional craftsmanship with modern standards to serve schools, clubs, tournaments, and retail markets. Our products are used and trusted by players across India. We prioritize quality, durability, and affordability.

  • ✔ Premium Carrom Boards
  • ✔ Cricket Bats: Leather & Tennis, Kashmiri Willow
  • ✔ Complete Sports Kits
  • ✔ Football, Badminton, Chess, and more
  • ✔ Customised Sports Uniforms

With dedication, experience, and customer satisfaction at our core, SABA SPORTS continues to grow while staying true to its roots.
