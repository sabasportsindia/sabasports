About SABA SPORTS

Founded in 2016, SABA SPORTS is built on over 16 years of collective experience in manufacturing and supplying premium sports gear.

Located in the sports hub of Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, our brand stands for precision, passion, and long-lasting performance. We’ve grown from humble roots into a trusted partner for 500+ retailers across India, and now proudly export our products worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower every athlete — from beginners to professionals — by delivering reliable, affordable, and high-quality sports equipment that fuels their passion and elevates their game.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized brand that symbolizes excellence in Indian sports manufacturing, and serves as the digital showroom of trust for dealers, coaches, and athletes worldwide.

Core Values

  • 🏆 Quality First — We never compromise on standards
  • 🤝 Reliability — What we promise, we deliver
  • 📈 Growth — We grow when our partners thrive
  • 🌍 Trust — Locally rooted, globally respected
